Types of salads

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed ingredients, frequently vegetables. They are typically served chilled or at room temperature, though some can be served warm. Condiments and salad dressings, which exist in a variety of flavors, are often used to enhance a salad.

About us

Welcome to India’s biggest salad brand, SaladO, where health meets taste. SaladO, the biggest salad brand in India, was conceived in a regular domestic kitchen in Nasik, where Riya Rohra, an enthusiast and co-founder, gave shape to and revolutionized salads. Riya used to extend her services to her large housing complex residents in Nashik, where she established the brand SaladO in August 2021. Kamlesh Rohra, her husband, has been a contributing partner in establishing the “Biggest Salad Brand,” SaladO. The passionate duo continues to contribute to their newfound love, SaladO. SaladO’s growth has been phenomenal since then. SaladO, being the largest salad brand, has been progressive and has more than 120 franchises across India.
